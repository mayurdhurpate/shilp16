Event Structure

Quriosity will consist of two rounds:
  • Online round:
    1. The online round will consist of logical reasoning puzzles.
    2. The duration of test will be of 1 hour comprising of 15 questions of varying difficulties and marking scheme.
    3. The test will be held in two slots:
      1. The first slot will be from 10:00 pm to 11:00 pm on 8th September, 2016.
      2. The second slot will be from 9:00 pm to 10:00 pm on 9th September, 2016.
    4. Each team consisting of maximum of two members can attempt the test in anyone of the two slots only.
    5. Top 10 percentile teams shall be selected for the onsite round, which will be held in IIT (BHU) Varanasi Campus.
    6. All the top performing teams of online round will be awarded goodies irrespective of their participation in on-site round.
  • On-site round:
    1. The structure of the onsite round to be held during Shilp’16 at IIT (BHU) Varanasi Campus, will be announced at a later stage.
