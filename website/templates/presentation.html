PAPER PRESENTATION


INTRODUCTION

"I believe in innovation and that the way you get innovation is you fund research and you learn the basic facts. "— (Bill Gates)

The story of every little mind. An idea pops up, wanders for a while, desires to be heard and is dumped out like a waste! Every innovative idea has a power to destroy the supposedly best option, all it needs is a platform to be heard and noticed, to be valued and appreciated.

A foundation for the tower, we present Shilp’16, with an aim to promote such ideas and research results in the field of Civil Engineering. Events that invite all enthusiastic scholars to pen down their ideas, build their dream structures and present their valuable work and suggestions.

It’s time to explore to the depth and present the best. Remember, there’s always a way to do it better, FIND IT!
