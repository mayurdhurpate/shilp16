COMPETITIONS

INTRODUCTION

Hydro-electricity is one of the most prominent sustainable form of electricity as hydroelectricity is generated using the kinematic energy from a turbine which receives the force from moving water. The above description makes it quite easy to understand that the turbine plays a very crucial role in generation of hydro-electricity. Modern turbines are very efficient but there is always room for improvement so Shilp’16 hosts torque to give a stage to all of you with those innovative ideas stacked up in your heads. In this event participants are supposed to design and build a hydro turbine to convert the mechanical energy from a falling stream of water to electrical energy. So participate in this event and learn and build something, not just for winning but for protecting the environment.

INTRODUCTION

Recently some anti-environmentalists have claimed that the Earth is actually in fine shape. Enviro is a case study about environmental degradation due to various reasons. You will be taught about the impacts of this degradation and the working principles of the new technologies that are being used nowadays to reduce pollution will be explained to you elaborately. You will get a great exposure to environmental engineering and a great deal of knowledge to develop an innovative thinking in this direction which is required to solve the environmental problem someday because the earth is not in good shape and we need all the help we can get to protect the environment so that one day we, unlike the false claims of the anti-environmentalists, may gladly declare that our Earth is in fine shape.

INTRODUCTION

Disasters or accidents can’t be avoided but improvisations can be made around us to live through them when they happen. In Seismica the participants will design a shock absorbing mechanism which can sustain maximum impulse when dropped from a given height with payload.
