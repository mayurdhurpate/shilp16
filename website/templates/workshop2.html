“ITS TIME TO INVESTIGATE ALL PAST MISTAKES AND FIND A WAY TO MAKE IT BETTER”

India is a large country.Nearly two- thirds of its area is earthquake prone.A large part of rural and urban structures are low-rise structures of one ,two or three storeys .Many of them may not be adequately designed from engineers trained earthquake engineering .if a structure is enough to resist the earthquake then such structures are called earthquake resistant structures.

It is not the shaking ground itself that claims lives ,it is the associated destruction of man-made structures and instigation of other natural disasters such as tsunamis,avalanches,and landslides that the need for a simple engineering approach to make such structures earthquake resistant and also at reasonably low cost.

Learning objective-to study the effects of earthquake and design earthquake resistant structures at its best.
