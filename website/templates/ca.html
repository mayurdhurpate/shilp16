CAMPUS AMBASSADOR

The Campus Ambassadorship Program by SHILP'16 is a nationwide programme which explores, innovates, shapes and exposes the students nationwide from various colleges as efficient managers and creative leaders. Members not only acquire leadership/ entrepreneurial qualities but also gain a social streak with a good character. We call upon our ambassadors to educate their constituents about the powerful role of socio-initiatives.

WHO SHOULD

“Don’t be surprised. Do be prepared”. SHILP AMBASSADORSHIPS are for young, proliferating and jubilant individuals who have effective mass communication skills and are influential. What we look in a Campus Ambassador is innovation, leadership, enthusiasm and creativity. We look for students who are leaders in their campus, leaders who can inspire their peers, collaborate with administrators and organize campus events to leave impact. It’s a chance to develop and grow as an individual while adding an incredible line to your resume. This is for those who are having keen interest in updating people about various shilp'16 events through public meets, announcements, notices, social-media, webpages, etc.

BENEFICIARIES TO CAMPUS AMBASSADORS

  • Reimbursement of Travel Expense (Sleeper Class), if the campus ambassador is able to get more than 10 participants from his college.
  • Free registration to the events of Shilp’16.
  • Shilp will provide goodies to the campus ambassadors.
  • Guest Lectures Passes : Free registration to attend the guest lectures of esteemed and renowned people in the field of Civil engineering.
  • Certification: The contribution of the Campus Ambassador will be rewarded by providing them Certificates for their help in promoting the festival.

    • PREREQUISITES

  • All applicants must be college/university students irrespective of their fields.
  • Should be thorough with their official procedures and Must be able to provide strong motivation.
  • Must be able to arrange, conduct meetings and should be able to handle the crowd.
  • Capable of appreciably representing the views and policies of an ambassador during campaigns.

    • A CAMPUS AMBASSADOR IS REQUIRED TO DO FOLLOWINGS THINGS

  • Should be engaged in increasing the number of participation from his/her college.
  • Is to take up the responsibility of promoting Shilp by forwarding posters and all the information about events etc. via mass mails in his/her college.
  • Shall help us in the online publicity of the festival thereby promoting Shilp in his/her college.
