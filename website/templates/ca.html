CAMPUS AMBASSADOR

The Campus Ambassadorship Program by SHILP'16 is a nationwide programme which explores, innovates, shapes and exposes the students nationwide from various colleges as efficient managers and creative leaders. Members not only acquire leadership/ entrepreneurial qualities but also gain a social streak with a good character. We call upon our ambassadors to educate their constituents about the powerful role of socio-initiatives.

WHO SHOULD

“Don’t be surprised. Do be prepared”. SHILP AMBASSADORSHIPS are for young, proliferating and jubilant individuals who have effective mass communication skills and are influential. What we look in a Campus Ambassador is innovation, leadership, enthusiasm and creativity. We look for students who are leaders in their campus, leaders who can inspire their peers, collaborate with administrators and organize campus events to leave impact. It’s a chance to develop and grow as an individual while adding an incredible line to your resume. This is for those who are having keen interest in updating people about various Technozion’14 events through public meets, announcements, notices, social-media, webpages, etc.

BENEFICIARIES TO CAMPUS AMBASSADORS

  • The Campus Ambassadors would be acknowledged by providing CERTIFICATES with ISO certification from SHILP IIT(BHU) VARANASI to them for successfully associating with the fest.
  • Special benefits and discounts in various coupons and entry cards to cultural and technical concerts and shows. Special/Wild card entry spaces for various general events.
  • Ambassadors gain valuable insights and experience throughout the SHILP'16.
  • Visibility of their engagement during SHILP'16 would be appreciated by letting Ambassadors have special exposure.
  • This programme would enhance many personal aspects of individual in terms of community services.

    • PREREQUISITES

  • All applicants must be college/university students irrespective of their fields.
  • Must be from age group (17-25) but should not be from final year of their course.
  • Must be holding some position in their college preferably (e.g.: Association/Student body member, club president, class representative, member of student welfare committee).
  • Should be thorough with their official procedures.
  • Must be able to provide strong motivation.
  • Must be able to arrange, conduct meetings and should be able to handle the crowd.
  • Capable of appreciably representing the views and policies of an ambassador during campaigns.
  • Must be consistent to work efficiently during his/her tenure.
  • No gender bar.
  • Good at English (preferably) or good at regional language (if local).
  • A maximum of 2 ambassadors are allowed from each college.
