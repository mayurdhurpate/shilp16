"Atithi Devo Bhava"

Well this motto runs in heart of each and every Indian. We at Shilp, IIT (BHU), Varanasi, welcome you at the ‘Abode of Lord Shiva’. With the overwhelming crowd arriving here, the hospitality of guests is our prime focus.
We, at Shilp are constantly striving to provide each and everyone of you the best facilities, fulfilling your needs, primarily for accommodation and your security. Along with accommodation facilities for our participants, we would be providing them both mess and canteen, serving them a variety of cuisine catering needs of every plate. We would pull out all the stops to make your stay comfortable, so that you can cherish these memories.
Eagerly awaiting for your arrival at Shilp’16. Feel free to give any suggestion or query.


From Airport to IIT(BHU):

BHU Campus is about 35 kms. from Varanasi (Babatpur) Airport. The taxi charges from Airport to BHU Campus are about INR 600/-.


From Varanasi Cantt. (Junction) Railway Station to IIT(BHU):

BHU Campus is about 10 kms. from Varanasi Cantt. railway Station. Round the clock Auto/ Taxi services are available from Varanasi Cantt. Railway Station to BHU (Lanka). The tentative charges are: for reserve auto Rs. 120-150/-, shared auto Rs. 15/- and for Taxi Rs. 300/- to 350/-.


From Mughalsarai Railway Station to IIT(BHU):

BHU Campus is about 20 kms. from Mughalsarai Railway Station. Round the clock Auto/ Taxi services are available from Mughalsarai Railway Station to BHU(Lanka). The tentative charges are: for reserve auto Rs. 200/-, shared auto Rs. 35/- and for Taxi Rs. 300/- to 350/-.




  • Accommodation will be provided from September 22nd evening.
  • You’re required to report at the hospitality desk at Dhanrajgiri Hostel as soon as you arrive at the campus.
  • All student participants must carry their valid College photo ID card. Others must bring their valid photo identity for the purpose of entry. People without valid photo identity card will not be allowed inside the campus during Shilp.
  • The fee for Shilp'16 registration is INR 300.
  • Registration includes:
    • Participation in any of the Shilp'16 events except Paper Presentation.
    • Passes for all guest lectures.
  • The fee for Paper Presentation registration is INR 500/Paper.
  • The fee for hospitality registration is INR 600.
  • Please bring 2 passport sized photographs.
  • Facilities provided:
    • Lodging from 22nd to 25th November .
    • Breakfast,lunch and dinner during the period of stay.
  • Security of laptops and other belongings will be the responsibility of participants only. Shilp will not be responsible for any loss or damage to the belongings of the participants.
  • Participants are to bring along all the material required for the respective events.
  • Kindly note that the accommodation is strictly on a shared basis and any requests for individual rooms or any complaints in this regard shall not be entertained.
  • Random checks would be made to avoid any illegal stay at the campus. Any team failing to produce their receipts of accommodation would be heavily fined and disqualified.
  • Alcohol, Cigarettes, drugs, any intoxicants, sharp objects and explosives of any kind are strictly prohibited inside the campus. Any other item if deemed unsafe will be prohibited. The decision of Shilp team will be final in case of any disputes.
  • There are SBI and Bank of Baroda ATMs inside the campus. There’s also an AXIS bank ATM just outside the campus, near Hyderabad Gate.
